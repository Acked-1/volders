About

It started early — music and electronics were two separate obsessions that slowly became one. A master's in electronics and IT gave the technical foundation, but the real education happened in front of machines with knobs.

After years of getting lost in DAW menus and preset libraries, the Korg Volca series opened a different path. Then came the Elektron Digitakt and Syntakt — and everything made sense. Buttons, knobs, sequencers. Hardware that pushes back.

Volders modules are the next step: translating that hardware-first mindset into open, free VCV Rack tools. Built for people who patch before they click. LFO-01 is the first one out — two more in development.
