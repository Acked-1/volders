About

I'm just someone who loves tinkering. New things, new challenges — that's what keeps it interesting. Music and electronics crossed paths at some point and I never looked back.

The Korg Volca series got me hooked on hardware. Then came the Elektron Digitakt and Syntakt, and it clicked — buttons, knobs, patch cables. No menus, no presets. Just hands-on.

Building VCV Rack modules is one of those things I love doing. LFO-01 is the first one out the door — made it for fun, shared it for free. More on the way whenever the tinkering takes me there.
